About Me

Hello! I'm Xavier Denson, a dedicated and passionate software development professional with a robust background in software support, quality assurance, and development. I hold a Bachelor of Science in Software Development and an Associate in Information Technology-Systems & Security from Green River College, as well as an Associate in Information Technology from Pierce College.

With over five years of diverse experience in the tech industry, I've honed my skills in software support, quality assurance, and development through various roles. Currently, I serve as a Software Support Specialist at Aero Controls, where I execute SQL scripts, manage support tickets, and ensure data integrity and system performance. My previous experience includes working as a Software Quality Assurance Engineer at Heritage Bank, where I significantly improved production times through automated testing, and as a Software Developer Intern at Costco, where I implemented key features and participated in Agile development processes.

My technical expertise includes proficiency in Java, JavaScript, SQL, and MySQL, along with hands-on experience with tools like Docker and Jira. I have a strong foundation in user acceptance testing, hardware/software troubleshooting, and UX research methods and design.

I'm committed to continuous learning and improvement, always seeking ways to enhance efficiency and user satisfaction in every project I undertake. My problem-solving skills and collaborative approach have consistently contributed to the success of the teams I've been part of.

In my free time, I enjoy exploring new technologies and staying updated with the latest trends in software development. I'm also an avid supporter of open-source projects and enjoy contributing to the tech community.

Feel free to connect with me on LinkedIn or reach out via email at xdenson253@outlook.com. Let's collaborate and create something amazing together!

Bea's Bakery Description

Overview

  • Bea's Bakery mobile application provides a delightful and intuitive experience for users to explore and order a variety of bakery items. The design ensures a seamless navigation through categories, customization options, and an easy checkout process.

Key Features

Home Screen

  • Promotions and Deals: Highlights daily deals and special offers with an attractive visual display.
  • Category Navigation: Allows users to quickly browse through different categories like cakes, cookies, and cupcakes with a swipe functionality.

Category Browsing

  • Visual Category Listings: Users can browse through tasty categories with appealing images representing each section, such as cookies, cupcakes, cakes, healthy options, and more.
  • Easy Navigation: Simple and clean interface to move between different categories and select items.

Order Customization

  • Customization Options: Users can personalize their orders by selecting from various sizes, icing preferences, and adding special requests or messages on cakes.
  • Interactive Elements: Engaging and interactive UI elements to choose the desired customization easily.

Order Confirmation

  • Payment Details: Clearly displays the breakdown of costs including the price of items, tips, and total amount.
  • Payment Methods: Multiple payment options including Credit/Debit cards, PayPal, and cash on delivery.
  • Order Preview: Visual preview of the order to ensure accuracy before confirming.

Order Summary

  • Summary Display: Summarizes the order details including items ordered, payment details, and estimated time for completion.
  • Edit and Confirm: Option to edit the order or proceed to confirm with a final visual confirmation.

Design Aesthetics

  • Color Scheme: A fresh and vibrant green and pink color palette to create a lively and appetizing visual appeal.
  • Typography: Playful yet readable fonts to maintain a balance between fun and usability.
  • Imagery: High-quality images of bakery items to entice users and enhance the browsing experience.

Pet Finder Description

Overview

  • The Pet Finder app is a user-friendly platform that connects pet lovers with their ideal companions. Featuring a visually appealing home screen with prominent images of pets and essential details, users can easily browse through various options. The minimalist navigation menu provides easy access to Home, Browse, Surprises, and Contact sections. Users can quickly find pets using the search and browse functionality, which categorizes pets by type. Additionally, the app offers a seamless shopping experience with pet-related products and a contact page for inquiries. Detailed pet pages with comprehensive information and images encourage users to adopt their new pets.

Home Screen (Desktop and Mobile)

  • Visually Appealing Design:
    • Prominent image of featured pets
  • Navigation Arrows:
    • Left and right arrows for scrolling through featured pets
  • Essential Pet Information:
    • Name and breed of each featured pet
    • "Read More" button for additional details
  • Social Media Icons:
    • Located at the bottom of the screen for easy sharing
  • Link to "About Us" Page:
    • Accessible at the bottom of the screen

Navigation Menu (Desktop and Mobile)

  • Hamburger Icon:
    • Access to the navigation menu
  • Slide-Out Menu:
    • Options for:
      • Home
      • Browse
      • Surprises
      • Contact
  • Minimalist Design:
    • Ensures easy navigation
    • Keeps user interface clean and uncluttered

Browse Screen (Desktop and Mobile)

  • Search Bar:
    • Allows users to find pets quickly

    • Options include:

      • Reptiles
